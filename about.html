Our Story

Founded by the Hamdi family from Tunis, Red Harissa brings the authentic taste of Tunisia to your neighborhood. Our recipes honor centuries-old traditions while celebrating bold spices and vibrant flavors.

From the bustling souks of Tunis to the coastal towns of Djerba, our menu tells a story of heritage, family, and Mediterranean warmth. Every dish is a celebration of our roots and our passion for culinary excellence.
